Who We Are

Built for
Those Who
Decide.

Pole 2 Pole exists for one audience: basketball decision-makers. College coaches evaluating international prospects they can't easily see in person. Pro front offices tracking talent years before the draft. Agents identifying clients before the competition does. International clubs that need a standard they can actually trust.

Most rankings are built for recruiting media and fan engagement. We are not. We are built for the coach who needs to know whether the prospect in France is worth a scholarship offer. The front office that wants to know if the Senegalese seven-footer is ready. The agent who wants to be two years early.

We have no shoe brand affiliation. No media parent company. No financial relationship with any team, program, league, or agency. Our credibility is the product — and we guard it accordingly.

Whether the prospect is at a powerhouse prep school or a youth academy in Belgrade, they are evaluated the same way. Same criteria. Same rigor. That's the only way a global ranking means anything to the people who rely on it.
